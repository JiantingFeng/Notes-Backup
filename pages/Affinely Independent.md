- The set $$\{v_1, \cdots, v_n\}$$ is **linearly independent** for any $$c_1, \cdots, c_n$$ not all $$0$$
	- $$\sum_{i=1}^nc_iv_i = 0\iff \forall i\in[n], c_i = 0$$
- It is called **affinely independent** if they are linearly independent and also
	- $$\sum_{i=1}^nc_i = 1$$
